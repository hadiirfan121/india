India: The Land of Diversity

India, officially known as the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area, the most populous country in the world, and the most populous democracy. Known for its rich history, diverse culture, and rapid technological growth, India is home to over a billion people speaking hundreds of languages across 28 states and 8 union territories.

To learn more about India, visit the official National Portal of India.
